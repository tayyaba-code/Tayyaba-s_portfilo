01 About

I'm a final-year Biomedical Engineering student at UET Lahore with three years of hands-on ML engineering experience across medical imaging, real-time computer vision, clinical prediction, and NLP.

My work spans the full pipeline — from raw clinical data through model training to Flask/FastAPI deployment — because a model that only exists in a notebook hasn't actually solved anything yet. I've validated outputs against ophthalmologist and radiologist review, built explainability layers with SHAP for clinical trust, and published three papers along the way, including one with Springer.

Lately I've been extending that pipeline into LLM tooling — LangChain, LangGraph, FastAPI, and local inference with Ollama — while keeping the same standard: rigour that survives contact with real data.
